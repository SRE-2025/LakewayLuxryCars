About the Company

Built for the cars that built you.

Lakeway Luxury Car Suites develops private automotive gallery residences in the Texas Hill Country — real property engineered around serious collections and the people who own them.

Why We Built It

A collection deserves an address.

Storage units insult the machines. Home garages run out of room. We watched collectors in Austin juggle warehouses, waitlists, and compromises — and built the answer on a private drive in Lakeway.

The first residence is finished and standing. Its twin is beside it. The land around them holds what comes next — released in phases, to the right owners.

What It's Designed For

Protect. Showcase. Enjoy.

Protect — museum-grade construction, climate, and gated security around the irreplaceable.

Showcase — gallery lighting, glass walls, and two-story volume that present the collection the way it deserves.

Enjoy — a full living level above the cars, with the best driving roads in Texas at your door. This is where the collection and the life happen in the same place.
